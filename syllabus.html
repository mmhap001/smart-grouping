Course Description

This Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) course is about learning how to design computer systems meant to be used by

people and that people enjoy interacting with. An HCI expert needs to be able to communicate effectively with end-users

and stakeholders; to generate, evaluate, critique, explain and justify their design ideas for user interfaces; to prototype

these design ideas; and to make tradeoffs between sometimes conflicting goals in order to create effective solutions

given necessary constraints.
